WHO IS SASSY HOLFORD

Sassi Holford is an award-winning British fashion designer who has been creating bridal
gowns, couture dresses and timeless evening wear for over 38 years. She aims to
produce effortlessly elegant designer pieces that champion craftsmanship and don’t cost
the earth. Every garment is handmade in England by Sassi and her incredible team of
talented artisans. Find out more about the story behind the brand. Her iconic designs are renowned for their superior cut and fit, which have attracted many
celebrity clients. This list includes Autumn Phillips’, who chose a Sassi Holford design for
her royal wedding. Sassi’s also responsible for some of Holly Willoughby’s most
memorable Dancing On Ice dresses. View our As Seen On gallery and magazine for more.
Sassi Holford’s much anticipated 2021 Bridal Collection will launch this spring. To be the
first to know when it’s live by signing up to our emails. In the meantime, explore the 2020
Enamour Collection, find your nearest stockist or book an appointment at our London
boutique.
